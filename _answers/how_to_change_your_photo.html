Adding your picture to email

Change your photo on Outlook.com

  1. At the top of the page, select your profile picture.
  2. Select Edit profile.
  3. Select Change picture.
  4. Select Browse and choose the picture you want to use.
  5. Drag or resize the image box and click Save.
